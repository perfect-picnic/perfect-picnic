FAQ & reviews

A few common questions about PerfectPicnic in Amsterdam. You can also leave a review after your picnic.

Where do you organise picnics?

We start in Vondelpark, Amsterdam. On request, we can explore other parks or locations in the city. We always send you a pin with the exact spot before your picnic.

How does payment work?

You first build your picnic and submit the form. We review your request and send you a secure payment link (e.g. iDEAL or card). Once paid, your booking is confirmed.

Can you handle allergies or dietary needs?

Yes. Please mention allergies, dietary preferences or restrictions in the form. We’ll adapt the basket and confirm what’s included before the picnic.

What if the weather is bad?

Amsterdam weather can be tricky. We’ll discuss options with you (rescheduling, adjusting time or partial refund) depending on the situation and forecast.

Is this good for tourists?

Absolutely. PerfectPicnic is ideal for visitors who want a cozy, local experience in Amsterdam without planning or shopping. We can communicate in English and Dutch.
